Can Huang is a Research Staff member working on power system planning and operation, renewable energy integration, and energy and customer data analytics.

Research Directions
======

- Power system planning and operation
- Renewable energy integration
- Energy and customer data analytics

Current Profile
======

Can Huang's work spans electric grid planning, grid operations, renewable integration, and data-driven analysis for energy systems. He has held research and engineering roles at Pacific Gas and Electric Company, Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory, and NARI Group Corporation.

Highlights
======

- Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from The University of Tennessee, Knoxville
- IEEE PES Prize Paper Award, 2025
- Associate Editor roles with IEEE Transactions on Smart Grid, IEEE Open Access Journal of Power and Energy, IEEE Transactions on Energy Markets, Policy and Regulation, and Journal of Modern Power Systems and Clean Energy
